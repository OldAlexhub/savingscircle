# Privacy Policy - Savings Circle

**App Name:** Savings Circle
**Developer:** Mohamed Gad / Old Alex Hub
**Package:** com.oldalexhub.savingscircle
**Effective Date:** June 30, 2026
**Contact:** info@oldalexhub.com

---

## 1. Overview

Savings Circle is a planning and bookkeeping tool for rotating savings groups. This policy explains what data is stored by the app and how advertising services may process data.

---

## 2. No Account Required

Savings Circle does not require you to create an account, register, or log in.

---

## 3. Savings Circle Data

The savings group information you enter in the app is stored locally on your device. This includes circle names, member names, payout amounts, contribution amounts, payment statuses, schedules, and reports.

Savings Circle does not upload this savings group information to our servers. We do not operate a backend service for storing your circle data.

---

## 4. Advertising

Savings Circle displays ads through Google AdMob. Ads are the app's revenue source.

Google AdMob may collect or receive information from your device to provide, measure, personalize, limit, and protect ads. This may include device identifiers such as the Android advertising ID, IP address, device and app information, approximate location inferred from network data, ad interactions, diagnostics, and related usage signals.

Google's use of data is governed by Google's policies, including:

- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Google advertising technologies: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Where required, the app uses Google's consent tools to ask for advertising consent before requesting ads.

---

## 5. Ad Choices

You can control ad personalization through your device or Google settings, including:

- Android Settings > Privacy > Ads
- Google Ad Settings: https://adssettings.google.com

Depending on your region, you may also see a consent or privacy options form for ads.

---

## 6. No Payment Processing

Savings Circle does not:

- Process, initiate, or receive real money payments
- Connect to banking systems, wallets, or payment networks
- Store financial account information, card numbers, or payment credentials
- Facilitate or guarantee money transfers between members

The app is a planning, scheduling, and bookkeeping tool only. Any actual money handling between savings group members occurs outside this app.

---

## 7. Permissions

Savings Circle may use internet and network access so Google AdMob can load and measure ads.

Savings Circle does not request access to:

- Camera
- Microphone
- GPS location
- Contacts
- Phone
- SMS
- External storage beyond its own app data

---

## 8. Third-Party Services

Savings Circle uses Google AdMob and Google's User Messaging Platform for advertising and consent management. We do not sell your savings circle data.

---

## 9. Children's Privacy

Savings Circle is not directed to children under 13. Do not use the app to enter information about children unless you have the legal right to do so.

---

## 10. How to Delete Your Data

You can delete your savings circle data in two ways:

1. Delete individual circles from within the app.
2. Clear all app data through the app settings or your Android device settings under Apps > Savings Circle > Storage > Clear Data.

Clearing local app data does not delete data that Google may have already processed for ads. To manage Google ad data, use your Google and Android ad settings.

---

## 11. Data Security

Savings circle records are stored on your device. We recommend using device-level security such as a PIN, password, fingerprint, or face unlock.

---

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be included in app updates. The effective date above will be updated accordingly.
