# Privacy Policy — Savings Circle

**App Name:** Savings Circle  
**Developer:** Mohamed Gad / Old Alex Hub  
**Package:** com.oldalexhub.savingscircle  
**Effective Date:** May 2025  
**Contact:** support@oldalexhub.com

---

## 1. Overview

Savings Circle is a local-only planning and bookkeeping tool for rotating savings groups (also known as gam3eya, tanda, susu, chama, ekub, committee, and similar community saving systems). This privacy policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

---

## 2. No Account Required

Savings Circle does not require you to create an account, register, or log in. The app works entirely offline on your device.

---

## 3. Data We Collect

**We do not collect any personal data.**

Savings Circle does not collect, transmit, store remotely, or share any of the following:

- Names or identities of savings group members
- Financial amounts, payout figures, or contribution data
- Device identifiers
- Location information
- Contact information
- Biometric data
- Usage analytics
- Crash reports (unless you explicitly choose to share them with your device OS)

---

## 4. Local Storage Only

All data you enter in the app — circle names, member names, payout amounts, payment statuses, and schedules — is stored **locally on your device** using Android's local storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device through the app.

---

## 5. No Payment Processing

Savings Circle does **not**:

- Process, initiate, or receive real money payments
- Connect to banking systems, wallets, or payment networks
- Store financial account information, card numbers, or payment credentials
- Facilitate or guarantee money transfers between members

The app is a planning, scheduling, and bookkeeping tool only. Any actual money handling between savings group members occurs entirely outside this app.

---

## 6. Permissions

Savings Circle requests **no Android permissions**. It does not access:

- Camera
- Microphone
- Location (GPS)
- Contacts
- Phone
- SMS
- External storage (beyond its own app data)
- Internet / network (no network connection is made)

---

## 7. No Ads

Savings Circle does not display advertisements and does not include any advertising SDK or ad network.

---

## 8. No Analytics

Savings Circle does not include analytics SDKs (such as Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.). No usage data is tracked or reported.

---

## 9. No Third-Party Services

Savings Circle does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or cloud platforms. All functionality is self-contained on your device.

---

## 10. Children's Privacy

Savings Circle does not target children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children. Since no data is collected at all, there is no special risk to child users.

---

## 11. How to Delete Your Data

You can delete your savings circle data in two ways:

1. **Delete individual circles** from within the app (Tracker tab → circle → delete).
2. **Clear all app data** via Settings → Data → Clear All Data, or via your Android device settings under Apps → Savings Circle → Storage → Clear Data.

---

## 12. Data Security

Since Savings Circle stores data only on your device and does not transmit it, your data is as secure as your device itself. We recommend using device-level security (PIN, fingerprint, etc.) to protect your data.

---

## 13. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be included in app updates. The effective date above will be updated accordingly.
